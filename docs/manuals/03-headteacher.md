# Shule SMS — User Manual
## Role: Headteacher

---

## 1. Introduction

As the **Headteacher**, you have broad oversight of all school operations. You can view and manage students, fees, attendance, exams, staff, and communications. You can also approve leave requests and manage class teacher assignments. You do not have access to the system administration panel — that is handled by the System Administrator.

---

## 2. Getting Started

### Logging In
1. Open the Shule SMS URL in your browser.
2. Enter your **email address** and **password**.
3. Click **Sign In**. You will land on the **Dashboard**.

### Navigation
Your sidebar contains:

| Menu Item | What it does |
|---|---|
| Dashboard | School-wide metrics and top defaulters |
| Students | Student records, enrolment, guardian contacts |
| Fees | Fee structures, invoices, payments, defaulters |
| Attendance | Daily register and attendance reports |
| Exams | Manage exams and view all results |
| Staff | Staff profiles, leave approvals, class assignments |
| Communications | Broadcast messages to parents |

---

## 3. Dashboard

The Dashboard shows a snapshot of school performance:

- **Total Students** — active enrolments
- **Fees Collected** — payments received this academic year
- **Outstanding Fees** — unpaid balances
- **Today's Attendance** — present percentage for today

Scroll down to see the **Monthly Revenue** chart and the **Top Fee Defaulters** list. You can send a WhatsApp fee reminder directly from the defaulters list by clicking **Send Reminder**.

---

## 4. Students

### Finding a Student
1. Click **Students** in the sidebar.
2. Type a name, student ID, or phone number in the search bar.
3. Filter by **Level** (Std 1–6 or Form 1–6) or **Status** (Active, Transferred, etc.).
4. Click a row to open the student's full profile.

### Enrolling a New Student
1. Click **+ New Student**.
2. Complete all sections:
   - **Personal details** — names, date of birth, gender
   - **Academic details** — level, stream, admission date
   - **Guardian details** — name, relationship, phone, email, WhatsApp
3. Click **Save**. A student ID is assigned automatically.

### Student Profile Tabs
| Tab | Contents |
|---|---|
| Overview | Personal details, guardian contacts |
| Fees | Invoices and payment history |
| Attendance | Monthly attendance summary and rate |
| Exams | All exam results, grades, and report card |

## 5. Fees

### Fee Structures
1. Click **Fees → Fee Structures** tab.
2. To add a new structure, click **+ Add Structure**:
   - Select **Academic Year**, **Level**, **Term**, and **Quarter**.
   - Enter the fee amount in TZS.
3. Existing structures can be edited by clicking the **edit icon** on each row.

> The school uses 2 terms per year: **Term 1** (Q1 = Jan–Mar, Q2 = Apr–Jun) and **Term 2** (Q3 = Jul–Sep, Q4 = Oct–Nov).

### Generating Invoices
1. Go to the **Invoices** tab.
2. Click **Generate Invoices**.
3. Select the **Academic Year**, **Term**, and **Quarter**.
4. Click **Generate** to create invoices for all students.

### Recording Payments
1. Find a student's invoice using the search or filters.
2. Click **Record Payment**.
3. Enter the **amount paid**, **method** (Cash, M-Pesa, Bank Transfer), and optional **reference**.
4. Click **Save**. A receipt number is generated.

### Defaulters
The **Defaulters** tab shows students with unpaid or partial invoices. Use the Term/Quarter filters to view a specific period. Click **Send Reminder** to trigger a WhatsApp or email notification to the guardian.

---

## 6. Attendance

### Marking Attendance
1. Click **Attendance** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Mark Attendance**.
3. Select **Date**, **Session** (Morning/Afternoon), **Level**, and **Stream**.
4. For each student, choose: **Present**, **Absent**, **Late**, or **Excused**.
5. Click **Submit**.

### Viewing Reports
Use the filter row to view records by date, level, stream, month, or status. The **Summary** view shows per-student attendance percentages.

The **Daily Summary** card at the top shows the school-wide rate for the selected date.

### Absentee List
Use the **Absentees** view (filter by date) to see all absent students with their guardian contact details. This is useful for making follow-up calls.

---

## 7. Exams

### Creating an Exam
1. Click **Exams → + Create Exam**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Title** — e.g. "Terminal Examination Form 3"
   - **Subject**, **Exam Type** (CA1, CA2, Mid-Term, Terminal, Mock)
   - **Level**, **Term**, **Quarter**
   - **Date** and **Maximum Score**
3. Click **Create**. The system notifies assigned subject teachers.

### Entering Marks
1. Click the **pen icon** on any exam row.
2. Enter marks for each student. Grades (A–F) are calculated automatically.
3. Click **Save All Marks**.

### Viewing Results
1. Click the **chart icon** on any exam row to open the Results page.
2. You can see individual marks, grades, class average, and highest/lowest scores.
3. Click **View Report Card** on any student to see their full report.

---

## 8. Staff

### Viewing Staff
1. Click **Staff** in the sidebar.
2. Browse the list or search by name.
3. Click a staff member to view their profile (designation, subjects, contact, leave history).

### Adding a New Staff Member
1. Click **+ Add Staff**.
2. Fill in: name, employee ID, designation, department, contract type, joining date.
3. Add subject assignments if the person is a teacher.
4. Click **Save**.

### Approving Leave Requests
1. In the Staff section, view the **Leave Requests** list.
2. Click **Approve** or **Reject** on each request.
3. Add an optional note before confirming.

### Class Teacher Assignments
1. Go to the **Class Assignments** tab.
2. Click **+ Assign Class Teacher**.
3. Select: teacher, level, stream, and academic year.
4. Click **Assign**. Only one teacher can be assigned per class per year.

### Disciplinary Incidents
1. Go to the **Discipline** tab.
2. View open incidents. Click an incident to see full details.
3. You can **refer** an incident to the Discipline Teacher or mark it as **Resolved**.

---

## 9. Communications

### Sending a Message
1. Click **Communications** in the sidebar.
2. Click **+ New Message**.
3. Select:
   - **Channel** — WhatsApp or Email
   - **Audience** — All Students, By Level, By Class, or Individual Student
   - **Subject** and **Message body**
4. Click **Send**.

For WhatsApp, a wa.me link is generated. Click **Open WhatsApp** for each recipient to send the message from your phone or computer.

---

## 10. Common Workflows

**Beginning of term:**
1. Verify the current Academic Year is set (check with System Admin if unsure).
2. Ask the Bursar to generate invoices for the new quarter.
3. Ask class teachers to confirm class registers are up to date.

**End of term:**
1. Confirm all marks have been entered (**Exams** section).
2. Print report cards from the Results page.
3. Check the Defaulters tab and send fee reminders.

**Staff leave approval:**
1. Go to **Staff → Leave Requests**.
2. Review dates against the academic calendar.
3. Click Approve or Reject.

**Following up on absentees:**
1. Go to **Attendance** and filter for today's date.
2. Use the **Absentees** view to see all absent students and their guardian phone numbers.
3. Call or send a WhatsApp message to each guardian.
