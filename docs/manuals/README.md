## System Overview

**URL:** Provided by your System Administrator  
**Login:** Email address + password  
**Password reset:** Contact the System Administrator

**Academic calendar:**  
- **Term 1** → Q1 (Jan–Mar) + Q2 (Apr–Jun)  
- **Term 2** → Q3 (Jul–Sep) + Q4 (Oct–Nov)

**Currency:** All amounts in Tanzanian Shillings (TZS)

**WhatsApp notifications:** Active via wa.me links — click to open WhatsApp with a pre-filled message  
**Email notifications:** Active if SMTP is configured (check with System Administrator)

## Common Questions

**I forgot my password.**  
Contact the System Administrator. They will reset it and give you a new temporary password.

**I can see a menu item but get "You are not authorised".**  
Your role may not have access to that feature. Contact the Headteacher or System Administrator to confirm your role assignment.

**I made a mistake entering marks.**  
Go to Exams, find the exam, click the pen icon, and correct the mark. Click Save All Marks again.

**A payment was recorded with the wrong amount.**  
Contact the Headteacher or Owner. Payment records are permanent to maintain an audit trail — a corrective entry may be needed.

**The system is slow or not loading.**  
Contact the System Administrator. They can check System Health to see if the database or background services have an issue.
