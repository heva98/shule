# Shule SMS — User Manual
## Role: Class Teacher

---

## 1. Introduction

As a **Class Teacher**, you are responsible for one class (level + stream) assigned to you for the academic year. In Shule SMS you can:

- Mark the daily attendance register **for your assigned class only**
- Enter exam marks for students in your class
- View student records and profiles
- Access the Dashboard

You do not manage fees, staff, or communications — those are handled by the Bursar, Headteacher, and Academic Teacher respectively.

---

## 2. Getting Started

### Logging In
1. Open the Shule SMS URL in your browser.
2. Enter your **email address** and **password**.
3. Click **Sign In**. You land on the **Dashboard**.

### Your Sidebar
| Menu Item | What it does |
|---|---|
| Dashboard | School-wide overview |
| Students | View student records |
| Attendance | Mark and view attendance (your class only) |
| Exams | Enter marks for your class's exams |

---

## 3. Your Class Assignment

Before you can mark attendance, the Headteacher or Academic Teacher must assign you to a class (level + stream) for the current academic year. If you cannot mark attendance, contact the Headteacher to confirm your assignment.

To see which class you are assigned to:
- Go to **Attendance → Mark Attendance**. The Level and Stream fields will reflect your assignment automatically.

---

## 4. Dashboard

The Dashboard shows school-wide statistics. As a Class Teacher, it is mainly informational:
- Total students enrolled
- Today's attendance rate
- Fees summary (read-only view)

---

## 5. Students

### Finding a Student
1. Click **Students** in the sidebar.
2. Search by name, student ID, or phone.
3. Filter by **Level** or **Status**.
4. Click any student to open their profile.

### Student Profile
You can view:
- **Overview** — personal details and guardian contacts
- **Attendance** — their full attendance history and monthly rate
- **Exams** — their results and report card

> You cannot edit student personal details. Contact the Headteacher if a record needs to be updated.

---

## 6. Attendance

This is your core daily responsibility. You are only permitted to mark attendance for your assigned class.

### Marking the Daily Register
1. Click **Attendance** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Mark Attendance**.
3. The **Level** and **Stream** are pre-set to your assigned class.
4. Confirm the **Date** (defaults to today) and **Session** (Morning or Afternoon).
5. For each student in the list, select their status:
   - **Present** — attended on time
   - **Absent** — did not attend
   - **Late** — arrived after the session started
   - **Excused** — notified absence (e.g. illness with guardian note)
6. Click **Submit**.

> If you need to correct a record you already submitted, you can re-submit the same date/session. The system will update the existing records.

### Viewing Your Class's Attendance
1. Go to **Attendance**.
2. Your class is pre-selected. Use the **Month** filter to review historical records.
3. The **Summary** view shows each student's attendance count and percentage.

### Understanding Status Types
| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Present | Student attended on time |
| Late | Student arrived after the register was called |
| Excused | Absence was notified in advance (guardian called, sick note provided) |
| Absent | Student did not attend and no notice was given |

> Marking a student **Excused** (rather than Absent) prevents an automatic absence alert being sent to the guardian. Use this when you have already been informed of the absence.

---

## 7. Exams

### Viewing Exams for Your Class
1. Click **Exams** in the sidebar.
2. Use the **Level** and **Quarter** filters to find exams for your class.

### Entering Marks
1. Find the exam in the list.
2. Click the **pen (marks entry) icon** on the exam row.
3. Enter each student's score in the marks column.
4. Grades (A–F) are calculated automatically as you type.
5. Click **Save All Marks**.

> You can only enter marks for students in your assigned class. If a student is missing from the list, they may not be enrolled in your class — check with the Headteacher.

### Viewing Results
1. Click the **chart icon** on any exam row to see the full results.
2. The results page shows all marks, grades, and the class average.
3. Click **View Report Card** on any student to see their cumulative results.

---

## 8. Common Workflows

**Morning routine:**
1. Take the register at the start of school.
2. Open **Attendance → Mark Attendance** (your class is pre-selected).
3. Mark each student and submit before 09:30.

**End of exam period:**
1. Collect marked scripts.
2. Open **Exams**, find the relevant exam, click the marks entry icon.
3. Enter all marks and click **Save All Marks**.
4. Check the Results page to confirm everything was saved correctly.

**A guardian asks about their child's attendance:**
1. Go to **Students**, find the student.
2. Open their **Attendance tab**.
3. Show the monthly summary and total percentage. The record is the official system record.

**Student is absent frequently:**
1. Open the student's profile → **Attendance tab**.
2. Note the percentage and missing dates.
3. Report to the Academic Teacher or Headteacher for follow-up.
